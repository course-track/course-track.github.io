Our Mission

Supply students with the resources they need so that they can accomplish their academic goals. Through consolidating information about college courses from a variety of helpful sources, students are better able to enroll in the classes that work best for them.

The Problem

Signing up for classes can be a challenge. Students have to enroll in specific classes to graduate on time and they want the classes that they do sign up for to suit them both academically and professionally. They also want to take advantage of enrolling in classes that are taught by the best professors that will make the class both enjoyable and challenge them appropriately. Today, most students use several different websites/platforms to find information about coursework so that they can make the best decisions on which classes to enroll in.

Trust

This application is built by students for students. Our team includes passionate individuals who want their peers to succeed in college by enrolling in the classes that are best suited for them. CourseTrack is free to use and simply combines publicly available information all in one place to help students in making their enrollment decisions effective and stress-free.
